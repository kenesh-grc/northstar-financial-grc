# Vendor Risk Management Program  
Northstar Financial Services

## 1. Purpose
To establish a structured approach for identifying, assessing, and monitoring third-party risks that may impact customer financial data or business operations.

## 2. Scope
Applies to all vendors that:
- Process or store customer financial data
- Provide cloud infrastructure
- Support critical business operations

## 3. Vendor Risk Classification

| Risk Tier | Description |
|-----------|------------|
| High | Access to sensitive financial data or critical systems |
| Medium | Limited access to internal systems |
| Low | No access to sensitive data |

## 4. Due Diligence Requirements

High-risk vendors must provide:
- SOC 2 Type II report or ISO 27001 certification
- Security questionnaire
- Data protection agreement
- Evidence of incident response capabilities

## 5. Ongoing Monitoring
- Annual vendor reassessment
- Review of SOC reports
- Monitoring for security incidents

## 6. Risk Treatment
Vendor risks may be:
- Mitigated through contractual controls
- Transferred via cyber insurance
- Accepted with executive approval

## 7. Governance Oversight
The GRC function reports third-party risk exposure quarterly to executive leadership.
