# Security Governance Charter  
Northstar Financial Services

## 1. Purpose
The purpose of this Security Governance Charter is to establish the structure, authority, and accountability for managing cybersecurity risk at Northstar Financial Services.

## 2. Scope
This charter applies to:
- All employees and contractors
- Cloud-hosted systems (AWS)
- Customer financial data and sensitive information
- Third-party service providers

## 3. Governance Objectives
- Protect customer financial data
- Maintain regulatory compliance
- Manage cybersecurity risks to acceptable levels
- Ensure accountability for security responsibilities

## 4. Roles and Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibility |
|------|---------------|
| Board / Executive Leadership | Provide oversight and approve risk appetite |
| CISO / Security Lead | Oversee cybersecurity strategy and risk management |
| GRC Analyst | Perform risk assessments and control monitoring |
| IT Operations | Implement and maintain technical controls |
| Business Units | Comply with security policies |

## 5. Risk Management Approach
Northstar Financial follows the NIST Cybersecurity Framework (CSF) and aligns security controls with ISO/IEC 27001 standards.

Risks are:
- Identified through assessments
- Evaluated using likelihood and impact
- Treated through mitigation, transfer, acceptance, or avoidance

## 6. Policy Framework
The security governance program includes:
- Information Security Policy
- Access Control Policy
- Incident Response Plan
- Third-Party Risk Management Policy

## 7. Review and Maintenance
This charter is reviewed annually or upon significant organizational or regulatory changes.
